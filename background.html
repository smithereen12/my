About me!

I am Nahom Yifru, currently pursuing an Associate in Database Programming and Administration at Bergen Community College. My journey is marked by a passion for problem-solving and a commitment to excellence. With a solid foundation in programming languages and database management, I bring a diverse skill set honed through practical experiences and volunteering roles.

Education:
My educational journey began at Montgomery College, where I laid the groundwork for my career with a focus on database programming and administration. Now, at Bergen Community College, I am furthering my knowledge and skills to excel in this dynamic field.

Work Experience:
My professional experience spans roles at prominent organizations such as S.T. Hubert’s Animal Welfare and the Humane Rescue Alliance, where I demonstrated my ability to thrive in fast-paced environments while prioritizing tasks efficiently. As a Maintenance Technician and Internal Transport Specialist, I ensured the well-being of animals while managing various responsibilities with precision and care. Prior to my work in animal welfare, I volunteered as a Laundry Assistant at the Worldwide Order of Jehovah’s Witnesses, where I honed my organizational skills and demonstrated my ability to work under tight deadlines.

Skills:
My proficiency extends beyond technical skills to encompass analytical thinking, strong oral communication, and a friendly, approachable personality. I am adept at programming languages such as Python, Java, and C++, as well as database management tools like MS SQL and data analysis platforms like R Studios. Additionally, my familiarity with tools such as PuTTY and Adobe software reflects my versatility and adaptability in diverse settings.
In addition, I've curated this website dedicated to showcasing my capabilities and experiences.

Committed to making meaningful contributions wherever I go, I am poised to leverage my skills and experiences to drive success and innovation in database programming and administration. With a passion for continuous learning and growth, I am excited about the opportunities that lie ahead in this dynamic field.
